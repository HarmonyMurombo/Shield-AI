Privacy Policy

At Shield AI, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our real-time weapon detection system.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: This may include your name, contact information, and user account details.
  • Video and Image Data: We process video feeds to detect weapons in real-time. This data is stored securely and used solely for weapon detection purposes.
  • Device Information: We collect device information such as IP addresses, device type, and browser information to improve service performance.
  • Log Files: We may collect log data about your interaction with the system for troubleshooting and performance improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Weapon Detection: The video data is analyzed to detect weapons and trigger alerts.
  • Alerts and Notifications: If a weapon is detected, we will send notifications via SMS, email, or other communication channels.
  • Service Improvement: Data may be used to improve the system's functionality and enhance user experience.
  • Legal Compliance: We may share your data as required by law or in response to legal requests.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored securely, and video footage is retained for up to 30 days unless you request its deletion. We use encryption to protect your data.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your data to third parties. However, we may share it with trusted service providers who assist us in delivering our service. Data may also be shared with law enforcement if required by law.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, for example, by remembering your preferences or for analytics purposes.

6. User Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You can also opt-out of notifications and data collection upon request.

7. Security Measures

We implement strict security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and access controls. We are committed to safeguarding your information.
